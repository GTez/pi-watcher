To Play a Twitch Video:
Go to Twitch's Directory and find a user you want to watch.
Then go to /twitch/play/BobRoss but replace BobRoss with twitch username

To watch a Youtube Video:
Go to Youtube.com and find a video you'd like to watch.
Copy the portion of the video after the ?v= part e.g. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M6FjLGqB-uc
Then head to /youtube/play/M6FjLGqB-uc replacing M6FjLGqB-uc with your video
